Pink Eye


Symptoms of Pink Eye:
Pink or red color in the white of the eye from visible blood vessels.
Swelling of the eyelids or thin layer that lines the white of the eye.
Crusting of eyelids or lashes, especially in the morning.

Cold and Flue


Symptoms of Cold and Flu:
The signs and symptoms of flu can include fever or feeling feverish/chills, cough, sore throat, runny or stuffy nose, muscle or body aches, headaches, and fatigue (tiredness).

Hair Loss


Symptoms of Hair Loss:
Acne, facial hair, or an abnormal menstrual cycle Weight gain or muscle weakness Intolerance to cold temperatures Fatigue

Allergies


Symptoms of Allergies:
Sudden swelling of the lips, mouth, throat, or tongue Difficulty breathing or very fast breathing Tightness in the throat or difficulty swallowing Skin, tongue, or lips that turn blue, gray, or pale Sudden confusion, drowsiness, or dizziness Fainting

Vomiting and Diarrhea


Symptoms of Vomiting and Diarrhea:
The main concern with vomiting and diarrhea is dehydration, which can occur when the body loses too much fluid. Signs of dehydration include: Decreased urine, Dark yellow color urine, Dry tongue and inside of the mouth, Dry lips, and Dry eyes with decreased or absent tears.

Sinus affection


Symptoms of Sinus affection:
Pain and pressure: Pain or pressure in your face, cheeks, eyes, forehead, or behind your eyes
Swelling: Swelling or tenderness around your eyes, cheeks, or forehead
Runny or stuffy nose: A blocked or runny nose, or thick, discolored mucus

Chronic Condition


Symptoms of Chronic Condition:
Common signs and symptoms include pain, difficulty with movement, excessive fatigue, and emotional or mood disorders.
